Mission Brief

Know Before You Go

This is a full-day production. Read this section carefully. Show up prepared. Show up on time. Show up sharp.

⏰

Schedule & Timing

  • Arrival: Be at The Moore by the designated call time — sharp
  • Duration: Full day experience — plan for 8-10 hours
  • Meals: Refreshments and food provided at key stops
  • Wrap: Final photos at Curated in the evening
  • Flexibility: Times between stops may shift — stay with the group
🎥

Production Crew

  • Photographer: Professional photographer at every stop
  • Drone: Drone operator for aerial convoy and formation shots
  • Follow car: Dedicated follow vehicle for rolling footage
  • Deliverables: Full edited photo + video gallery post-event
  • Direction: Follow the photographer's lead at each stop
⚠️

Ground Rules

  • License: Valid driver's license required — no exceptions
  • Speed: All speed segments are controlled and coordinated
  • Traffic laws: Follow all local traffic laws at all times
  • Convoy: Stay with the group — no solo detours
  • The cars: Treat the Ferraris with respect. You break it, you buy it.
✈️

Seaplane & Padel

  • Padel: Reserve Padel on Watson Island — racquets provided
  • Seaplane: Scenic flight retracing the full rally route
  • Same location: Padel courts are at the seaplane base
  • Weather: Seaplane flight subject to conditions
  • Weight: Weight limits may apply — confirmed on RSVP
